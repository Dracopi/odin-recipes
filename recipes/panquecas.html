Bearcakes!

Everyone loves pancakes, and everyone loves bears, so why not have both?!

Ingredients

  • 2 bears
  • 500 gr white flour
  • 1kg butter
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 cup of sesame

Steps

  1. keep the bears with you
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Wipe the bears' faces clean
  4. Dip the face of the bears in the batter (we recommend having two bears for more speed)
  5. Drop the batter into the pan and cook!
