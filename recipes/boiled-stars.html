Boiled Stars

A typical breakfast for intergalactic beings

Ingredients

  • two to three stars
  • dark matter
  • An eternal source of energy
    • It could be a fire that has been burning since creation.
    • or the inner fire that drives you
  • A bottomless plate

Steps

  1. fill your bottomless dish with dark matter (make sure it's suitable for the heat!)
  2. place it on top of your preferred heat source
  3. wait 10 to 30 millennia
  4. eat it!
